The Costly Challenges of the Last Mile

High Costs & Delays

Last-mile delivery in Vietnam is hugely inefficient, often accounting for **up to 53%** of total logistics costs. Traffic congestion and crowded elevators add 10-20+ minutes per delivery.

Security & Disputes

Lack of reliable recipient verification leads to lost packages, mistaken deliveries, and constant conflicts between residents and couriers.

Safety & Shortages

Urban staff shortages are common, and the demand for contactless, hygienic delivery has become an essential health and safety requirement.

PUDU Innovation: Navigating Vertical Cities

1.

AI-Powered Multi-Sensor Navigation

PUDU utilizes advanced systems like **V-SLAM and 3D LiDAR** for precise real-time mapping. This multi-sensor fusion allows the robot to safely and confidently navigate complex interiors, avoiding obstacles like people, furniture, and pets.

2.

Seamless Elevator Integration (Game-Changer)

Our proprietary system allows the robot to **communicate directly with the building's management system** to call, wait for, and ride the elevator independently. This ensures true, autonomous multi-floor delivery to any unit.

3.

Real-Time Tracking & Reporting

Full transparency is provided via a dedicated mobile application, allowing residents to monitor the robot's exact location and status from the moment it enters the building until delivery.
