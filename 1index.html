The Costly Challenges of the Last Mile

High Costs & Delays

Last-mile delivery in Vietnam is hugely inefficient, often accounting for up to 53% of total logistics costs. Traffic congestion and crowded elevators cause delays and frustration for both couriers and residents.

Security & Disputes

Lack of reliable recipient verification leads to lost packages, mistaken deliveries, and constant conflicts between residents and couriers.

Safety & Shortages

Urban staff shortages are common, and the demand for contactless, hygienic delivery has become an essential health and safety requirement.
